Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: 8 July 2026

We want you to be satisfied with your Cheradip subscription. This policy covers the Cheradip coding assistant, whose payments are handled by Paddle.com, our Merchant of Record; refunds are issued through Paddle. The AI Language Tutor Android app is billed through Google Play and is subject to Google Play's refund policy.

14-day money-back guarantee. If you are not satisfied with a new paid subscription, you may request a full refund within 14 days of the initial purchase.

1. Subscriptions

  • Subscriptions renew automatically until cancelled.
  • You can cancel at any time from the Cheradip extension (Settings → Billing) or via the billing portal link on your receipt. Cancellation stops future renewals; your plan remains active until the end of the current billing period.
  • Except for the 14-day guarantee above, charges for the current period are generally non-refundable, and we do not provide prorated refunds for partial periods or unused requests.

2. Free trial

Where a free trial is offered, you will not be charged during the trial. If you do not cancel before the trial ends, the plan converts to a paid subscription and your payment method is charged.

3. Pay-as-you-go (PAYG)

PAYG charges reflect usage you have already consumed beyond your plan quota and are generally non-refundable, except where required by law or in cases of a proven billing error.

4. How to request a refund

Email support@cheradip.com from your account email with your order/receipt number and the reason for the request. You can also use the support link on your Paddle receipt. Approved refunds are returned to your original payment method; processing times depend on your bank or card issuer.

5. Exceptions

We may decline refunds in cases of policy abuse (for example, repeated refund requests after substantial usage) or where prohibited by applicable law. Nothing in this policy limits statutory consumer rights that apply to you.

6. Contact

Cheradip — support@cheradip.com.
