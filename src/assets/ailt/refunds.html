Refund & Cancellation Policy

Cheradip AI Language Tutor (Android)

Last updated: 16 July 2026

Subscriptions for AI Language Tutor are sold through Google Play. Google is the merchant of record for those purchases. Refunds and cancellations follow Google Play’s policies in addition to this page.

1. Cancel a subscription

  • In the App: Profile → Manage subscription (opens Google Play).
  • Or open Google Play Store → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions.
  • Select AI Language Tutor / Cheradip and cancel.
  • Direct link: Play subscriptions for this app
  • You keep access until the end of the current paid period unless Google states otherwise.

1b. Delete your account

See cheradip.com/ailt/delete-account. Deleting your Cheradip account does not automatically cancel Play renewals — cancel the subscription first (or immediately after).

2. Request a refund

Use Google Play’s refund tools where available (for example Play order history), or contact us at support@cheradip.com with your order number. Eligibility and timing are determined by Google Play’s rules and applicable law.

3. Free trial

If a free trial is offered, you will not be charged during the trial. Cancel before the trial ends to avoid conversion to a paid subscription.

4. Coding Agent (separate product)

Refunds for the Cheradip AI Coding Agent (editor extension) are described at cheradip.com/aicodingagent/refunds.
