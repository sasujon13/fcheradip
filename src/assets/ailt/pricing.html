Each plan meters two things: line edits (replacements + insertions) and requests. Line edits are the primary limit; requests are the fallback. On any paid plan you can enable pay-as-you-go to keep working past your quota at $0.02 per request or $0.02 per 10 line edits — you're billed whichever is higher, never both. When your overage reaches the cost of the next plan, we ask you to settle it or upgrade — no month-end surprises.

Payments are processed securely by Paddle, our authorised reseller and Merchant of Record. Prices are in USD and exclude any applicable sales tax/VAT, which Paddle calculates at checkout. You can also subscribe from the Cheradip extension (Settings → Billing).
