This Privacy Policy explains how Md. Shafikul Islam, operating under the brand Cheradip (“we”, “us”, or “Cheradip”), collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the AI Language Tutor Android application and related App API services at https://cheradip.com/ailt/api/ (together, the “App”). By installing or using the App, you agree to this Policy.

This page applies only to the AI Language Tutor Android app. The separate Cheradip AI Coding Agent has its own privacy policy at cheradip.com/aicodingagent/privacy.

1. Summary (what Google Play reviewers need)

  • We collect account details (email, name, hashed password) when you sign up.
  • We process scanned images and text you choose to learn from; documents primarily stay on your device unless you sync or send text for AI features.
  • Optional AI features send selected text (and, for some modes, related context) to our servers and/or third-party AI providers.
  • Microphone audio is used for speaking practice and voice calibration on device; we do not sell voice recordings.
  • Subscriptions and payments are handled by Google Play; we do not receive your full card number.
  • We do not sell personal information and we do not show ads in this App.
  • You can request account and data deletion by emailing support@cheradip.com.

2. Information we collect

2.1 Information you provide

  • Account information — email address, display name / username, and a hashed password when you create an account.
  • Profile preferences — practice languages (up to 3), tutor voice preference (teen male/female), app UI language, and similar settings.
  • Learning content you create — scanned or imported documents, typed practice answers, notes, and saved activities that you generate while using the App.
  • Support messages — information you send when contacting support (email content and related metadata).
  • Referral codes — if you use or share a referral / promo code.

2.2 Information collected automatically

  • Device & app identifiers — a device identifier used for guest AI limits, trial timing, and abuse prevention; app version; approximate device/OS information needed to operate the App.
  • Usage & diagnostics — feature usage (for example AI request counts), subscription entitlement status, error/crash diagnostics, and timestamps needed to run quotas and improve reliability.
  • Network logs — standard server logs (IP address, request time, endpoint) when the App talks to our App API.

2.3 Permissions and on-device data

Depending on features you use, the App may request:

  • Camera — to scan books, worksheets, and notes.
  • Photos / media / files — to import images or documents you select.
  • Microphone — for speaking practice (“Say”) and voice calibration.
  • Internet — for account login, language pack downloads, subscription verification, and optional cloud / Home AI features.
  • Notifications (if enabled) — for optional reminders or account messages.

On-device OCR, dictionary packs, and many learning features work offline. Scanned documents are stored locally on your device by default.

2.4 Payment information

Paid subscriptions (after the free trial) are purchased through Google Play Billing. Google processes your payment method. We receive purchase tokens / entitlement data (for example product ID, purchase state, expiry) so we can unlock Pro/Plus features. We do not store your full credit or debit card number.

3. How we use information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App (scan, read, practice, grammar, offline packs).
  • Create and authenticate your account; reset passwords via email OTP.
  • Sync learning history, referrals, and subscription status when you are signed in.
  • Run optional AI features (explanations, structure, practice feedback) using our App API and/or Home AI.
  • Enforce trial limits, guest AI request limits, and subscription entitlements.
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and technical issues; comply with law.
  • Respond to support requests.

We do not use your personal learning content to show third-party advertising, and the App contains no ads.

4. AI processing and third parties

When you use online AI features, selected text (and limited related context needed for the request) may be sent to:

  • Cheradip App API — https://cheradip.com/ailt/api/
  • Cheradip Home AI — https://ai.cheradip.com (when configured / available)
  • Third-party AI model providers acting as processors on our behalf (for example cloud LLM providers), solely to generate the response you requested

We share only what is needed to fulfil your request. We do not sell your content. Third-party providers process data under their own terms and security controls. Offline dictionary meanings come from language packs stored on your device.

5. When we share information

We may share information with:

  • Google Play / Google — billing, Play integrity, and related Google services required to distribute and monetise the App.
  • Hosting and email providers — to run our servers and send account emails (for example password reset codes from noreply@cheradip.com).
  • AI providers — as described in section 4.
  • Authorities — if required by applicable law or to protect users, rights, or safety.

We do not sell personal information.

6. Data retention

  • On-device data — remains until you delete it in the App or uninstall the App (subject to your device’s storage behaviour).
  • Account data — kept while your account is active and for a reasonable period afterward for security, legal, and accounting needs.
  • Server logs & usage meters — retained for a limited operational period.
  • Purchase records — retained as needed to honour entitlements and meet legal obligations.

7. Your choices, access, and deletion

  • You can edit profile and learning preferences in the App.
  • You can limit AI use by staying offline or using offline packs only.
  • You can cancel a subscription in Google Play → Subscriptions.
  • To request access, correction, or deletion of your account and associated server data, email support@cheradip.com from your account email and include “Delete my AI Language Tutor account” in the subject. We will verify ownership and process the request within a reasonable time, except where we must retain limited records for legal or fraud-prevention reasons.

8. Security

We use technical and organisational measures appropriate to the App, including HTTPS for network traffic, hashed passwords, and access controls on our servers. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

9. Children

The App is intended for a general audience learning languages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 for the purpose of selling data or behavioural advertising. If you believe a child has provided personal data inappropriately, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

10. International transfers

We are based in Bangladesh. Servers, email, Google Play, and AI providers may process data in other countries. Where required, we take steps to use appropriate safeguards for such transfers.

11. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the App after an update means you accept the revised Policy. Material changes may also be noted in the App or on this page.
