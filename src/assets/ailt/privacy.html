Privacy Policy

Last updated: 8 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Cheradip ("we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use Cheradip's products — the AI Language Tutor app and the Cheradip AI coding assistant — and related services (the "Service"). By using the Service you agree to this Policy.

1. Information we collect

Account information

When you create an account we collect your name, email address, and a hashed password. We store session tokens to keep you signed in.

Billing information

Payments are processed by Paddle (our Merchant of Record). We do not receive or store your full card details. We receive limited transaction data such as your plan, payment status, billing country, and a transaction identifier to manage your subscription.

Usage data

We record product usage such as the number of AI requests, quota consumption, feature usage, timestamps, and diagnostic/error logs, so we can operate the Service, enforce quotas, and improve reliability.

Content you submit

To fulfil your requests, the Service processes the code, files, prompts, and context you choose to send. This content is used to generate responses and is not sold.

2. How we use information

  • To provide, maintain, and secure the Service;
  • To authenticate you and manage sessions and subscriptions;
  • To meter usage and apply plan quotas and pay-as-you-go;
  • To provide support and respond to enquiries;
  • To detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, or technical issues;
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. AI processing & third parties

To generate responses, the content you submit may be transmitted to third-party AI model providers acting on our behalf. We share only what is necessary to fulfil your request. We do not use your private code to train our own foundation models. Third-party providers process data under their own terms and security measures.

4. Sharing of information

We share information with service providers who help us run the Service, including Paddle (payments), hosting and infrastructure providers, and AI model providers. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights, users, or the public. We do not sell your personal information.

5. Data retention

We keep account and billing records for as long as your account is active and as required for legal, accounting, or fraud-prevention purposes. Usage logs are retained for a limited period needed for operations and security. You may request deletion of your account (see below).

6. Security

We use technical and organisational measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit, hashed passwords, and access controls. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at support@cheradip.com. For payment data held by Paddle, you can also contact Paddle directly.

8. International transfers

We operate globally, and your information may be processed in countries other than your own. Where required, we take steps to ensure appropriate safeguards for such transfers.

9. Children

The Service is not directed to children under 18, and we do not knowingly collect their personal information.

10. Changes

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date.

11. Contact

Cheradip — support@cheradip.com.
