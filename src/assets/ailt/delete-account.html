Account

Delete your account

Cheradip AI Language Tutor — request permanent deletion of your account and associated server data.

Last updated: 18 July 2026

Developer: Md. Shafikul Islam (Cheradip), Bangladesh
App package: com.cheradip.ailanguagetutor
Support: support@cheradip.com

Google Play requires that users can request deletion of their account and associated data. Use one of the methods below. Deleting your Cheradip account removes login credentials and synced server data for this App. It does not automatically cancel a Google Play subscription — cancel billing in Google Play so you are not charged again.

1. Cancel your subscription (recommended first)

  1. Open the Google Play Store on your Android device.
  2. Tap your profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions.
  3. Select AI Language Tutor / Cheradip and tap Cancel subscription.
  4. Or open: play.google.com/store/account/subscriptions?package=com.cheradip.ailanguagetutor
  5. In the App you can also go to Profile → Manage subscription, which opens the same Google Play page.

You typically keep access until the end of the current paid period. Refunds follow our refund policy and Google Play’s rules.

2. Delete account in the App (fastest)

  1. Open AI Language Tutor and sign in.
  2. Go to Profile.
  3. Tap Delete account.
  4. Confirm with your password. This permanently deletes your Cheradip account for this App on our servers and signs you out.

3. Delete by email (if you cannot use the App)

Send an email from the same address you used to register:

  • To: support@cheradip.com
  • Subject: Delete my AI Language Tutor account
  • Body: Include your account email / username and confirm you want permanent deletion.

We verify ownership and process the request within a reasonable time (typically within 30 days). We may retain limited purchase or fraud-prevention records where required by law.

4. What is deleted

  • Account credentials and profile on Cheradip App API servers
  • Session tokens (you are signed out everywhere)
  • Synced learning activity records tied to your user id
  • Referral balance records tied to your user id (where applicable)

Usually kept (as needed): anonymised or limited billing/purchase tokens for legal, accounting, or dispute purposes; device-level trial counters that are not your account; on-device files you created until you clear app data or uninstall.

5. What deletion does not do

  • Does not cancel Google Play renewals by itself — cancel in Play (section 1).
  • Does not delete a separate Cheradip product account (for example the AI Coding Agent extension account).
  • Does not remove content already stored only on your device until you uninstall or clear storage.
