Pay-as-you-go — no Price ID needed.
PAYG is an add-on on Pro, Plus, or Business (enabled in the extension settings). When a team exceeds their monthly quota, usage is metered on our server ($0.02 per request or $0.02 per 10 line edits — whichever bill is higher). When they choose to pay, the API creates a one-off Paddle checkout for the exact amount due. You do not create a separate PAYG product in Paddle.

Set the webhook URL in Paddle to https://cheradip.com/ailt/api/subscription/webhook.
