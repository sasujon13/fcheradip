Before you write

These details speed up a reply:

  • Account email / username (extension account — not the Android Language Tutor phone login)
  • Editor: VS Code (+ version)
  • Extension version (Extensions → Cheradip)
  • Plan (Free / Pro / Plus / Business) and whether PAYG is enabled
  • What you expected vs what happened, plus any error text from the Output → Cheradip panel

Billing help

  • Upgrade / subscribe — Extension Settings → Billing, or Pricing
  • Cancel renewal — Settings → Billing, or the portal link on your Paddle receipt
  • PAYG overage — settle from Settings → Billing → Pay now (one-off Paddle checkout)
  • Invoice / tax — handled by Paddle; check your receipt email or contact them via the receipt link
  • Refund — Refund policy · email support within 14 days of a new paid subscription when eligible
